Power Smoothie

Description

This healthy smoothie is packed with protien, iron, potassium, and calcium! It is filling for meal replacement and packed with nutrients to ensure a well balanced diet! For me, this treat curbs some of my cravings for shakes and icecream, which is an added bonus!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 cups of unsweetened vanilla oat milk
  • 1 handful of baby spinach
  • 2 ripe bananas
