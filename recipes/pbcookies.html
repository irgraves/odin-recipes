Peanut Butter Cookies

An easy treat with only 3 ingredients!

Since the dawn of peanut butter, man strived to insert it into a cookie. After long deliberation, the top scientists have finally made this possible. After much convincing they are now allowing me to share this with you today!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. mix peanut butter, sugar, and the egg using an electric mixer until smooth
  3. roll into 1 inch balls and place 1 inch apart on an ungreased baking sheet
  4. flatten with a fork, making a criss cross pattern
  5. bake until edges are firm, around 10 minutes, and let cool
