Ramen

Description

This chicken ramen recipe is a simple and quick way to enjoy restaurant style ramen at home! Ramen is a type of Japanese noodle soup usually made with an umami broth of stewed bones and chewy alkaline noodles. This recipe is designed to make it much simpler for people with busy lives!

Here's What You Need

Broth:

  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 6 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 inches of ginger
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup low sodium soy sauce

Chicken

  • 1 teaspoon low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 boneless and skinless chicken breast

Other:

  • 2 eggs
  • 5.3 oz dry egg noodles
  • 1 green onio
