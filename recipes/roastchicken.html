Roast Chicken

A culinary staple since olden times

Since the discovery of flightless birds, the chicken has been a staple in nearly all diets worldwide. Delicious and nutritious, roast chicken provides needed sustinance.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • salt and black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 stalk celery, leaves removed

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. place chicken in pan
  3. season generously with salt, pepper, and onion powder inside and out
  4. place 3 tablespoons of butter inside the cavity, and arrange the rest on the outside
  5. cut and arrange celery inside the cavity
  6. bake in preheated oven for approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until juices run clear
  7. remove from oven, baste with drippings, and enjoy after 30 minutes
