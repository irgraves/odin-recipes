Grilled Cheese

Description

A delicious savory sandwich consisting of bead and cheese! An absurdly simple concept that has stood the test of time in American cuisine for decades. Perfectly paired with a soup of your choice! This gooey treat is great dunked or eaten on its own!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite cheese
  • 2 pieces of white bread
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. Preheat a cast iron skillet over medium heat on the stove top
  2. Spread your butter on one side of each bread slice
  3. Place the cheese between the bread slices with the butter on the outside
  4. Cook on each side, flipping only when the bottom side becomes golden brown
  5. Cut in half and enjoy!
