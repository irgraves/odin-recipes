Popcorn

You may be asking yourself "Isn't corn yellow?" Not anymore!

Since the dawn of time, corn has been thought of as yellow, but if you add some oil to it, it gets fluffy and white. Add some other stuff and you have a gourmet feast!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup unpopped popcorn
  • salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Steps

  1. place popcorn in a sealed paper bag
  2. microwave on high until poppin slows, about 2 minutes
  3. season with salt and drizzle with oil
  4. shake until seasoning is distributed
